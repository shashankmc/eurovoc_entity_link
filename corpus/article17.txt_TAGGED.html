article 17 designation of the competent authority 1. each member state shall designate one or more entities as the competent authority for the tasks referred to in article 18. 2. where a member state designates more than one entity as a competent authority it shall: (a) clearly define the areas of competence of each competent authority in terms of responsibilities; (b) establish appropriate coordination mechanism between those entities to ensure the effective oversight of all organisations and persons subject to this regulation.
